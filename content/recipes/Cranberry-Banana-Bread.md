---
title: Cranberry Banana Bread
author: Sabrina
source: ""
categories: [Breads & Muffins]
cookTime: 0
prepTime: 0
preheat: "350"
date: "2006-08-18 23:25:24"
ingredients:
- sugar: 1 cup
- margarine, softened: 1/2 cup
- mashed bananas: 1 cup
- milk: 1/4 cup
- eggs: "2"
- flour: 2 cups
- baking powder: 2 tsp
- craisins: 1-170 g pkg
- chopped nuts: 1/4 cup
directions:
- Cream together sugar and margarine.
- Add eggs and bananas.  Mix together flour and baking powder.  Add alternately with
  milk to sugar mixture.
- Fold in craisins and nuts.
- Pour into greased loaf pan.  Bake 1 hour 10 minutes.
notes:
- Instead of loaf, will make 12 muffins.
---
