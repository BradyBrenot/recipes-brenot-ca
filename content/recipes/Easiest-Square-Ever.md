- name: Directions
  steps:
  - Mix milk, crackers and 3/4 of the skor bits together.
  - Pack into greased 9 x 13 pan.
  - Bake for 15 - 20 minutes.
  - Cool. Ice with cream cheese icing and sprinkle with remaining skor bits.
